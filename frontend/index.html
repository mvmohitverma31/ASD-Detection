WHAT IS ASD

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a developmental condition that can affect communication, behavior, social interaction, and the way a person experiences the world around them. It is not something to be feared, and it does not mean something is wrong with a person. Many people with ASD live meaningful, successful, and deeply fulfilling lives with the right understanding and support. Early awareness can be helpful because it allows individuals and families to better understand strengths, needs, and the kinds of support that may make daily life easier and more comfortable.
